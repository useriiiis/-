📚 Elliott Wave Theory Quick Guide
Impulse Waves (1→2→3→4→5): The main trend movement. Wave 3 is usually the longest. Wave 4 cannot enter Wave 1 territory.
Corrective Waves (A→B→C): Counter-trend movement after impulse completes. Usually 50-78.6% retracement of waves 1-5.
Fibonacci Targets: Wave 3 = 1.618× Wave 1; Wave 5 = 0.618× to 1× of Wave 1+3; Wave C = 1× Wave A.

📚 What is DCA (Dollar-Cost Averaging)?
DCA means investing a fixed amount at regular intervals, regardless of price. This smooths out volatility - you buy more shares when prices are low and fewer when prices are high. Used by retail investors, 401k plans, and even Warren Buffett's recommendation for most people.

⚠️ Note: Past performance does not guarantee future results. This simulator uses historical price data only and does not include dividends, taxes, or stock splits' adjusted prices automatically (Yahoo's adjusted close handles splits but not always perfectly).

📊 MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)
EMA(12) − EMA(26), Signal=EMA(9). Detects trend changes via crossovers.
⚡ RSI (Relative Strength Index)
14-period momentum. >70=overbought, <30=oversold. Divergence is strongest signal.
📈 KDJ (Stochastic Oscillator)
中国常用动量指标。K&D 在底部金叉=买; 顶部死叉=卖. J<0 极度超卖, J>100 极度超买.
📉 Bollinger Bands (布林带)
20-day MA ± 2σ. Squeeze = breakout coming. Touching upper = strong, touching lower = weakness.
📊 Volume Analysis (放量/缩量)
放量 = volume > 2× MA20 (institutional activity). 缩量 = consolidation. OBV confirms trend.
📐 Moving Averages (均线系统)
MA5/10/20/50/200. Golden Cross (50↑200) = 大牛市. Death Cross (50↓200) = 大熊市.
🧩 缠论 (Chan Theory) Simplified
缠中说禅理论。识别"笔"(strokes) 和"中枢"(consolidation zones)。中枢突破=trend continues, 反向=reversal.
